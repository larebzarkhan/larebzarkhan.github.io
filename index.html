01 — About

The person
behind the research

Hello, my name is Lareb, and I am an optical network engineer who is passionate about driving innovation in the operation and management of optical networks. I am originally from Pakistan and currently a Network Production Engineer at Meta in London.

My research focuses on using machine learning to give optical networks more autonomy. I have specifically worked on devising effective failure management strategies to ensure uninterrupted and high-quality internet services for end-customers. By enabling networks to operate autonomously, I believe we can dramatically improve performance and the end-user experience.

When I'm not doing research, you can find me exploring new places and cultures as an occasional traveller. I'm also an avid reader of inspirational fiction and non-fiction literature, always seeking fresh perspectives and insights.
